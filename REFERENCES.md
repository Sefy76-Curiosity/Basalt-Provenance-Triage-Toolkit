This toolkit utilizes reference datasets and classification parameters derived from the following peer-reviewed studies:
üèõ Archaeological & Provenance Studies
    Gluhak et al. (2016, 2022): Primary data source for the Tel Hazor Iron Age basalt workshop demo data and northern Levantine geochemical fingerprints.
    Hartung, U. (2017): Geochemical benchmarks for Egyptian basalt flows, specifically utilized in the Literature Comparison module to distinguish Nile Valley sources from Levantine imports.
    Ebeling, J. R., & Rosenberg, D. (2015): Basalt vessel typology and provenance studies in the Southern Levant, used for the "Rosenberg" benchmarks in the literature comparison plugin.
    Sefy Levy (2026): The Richat-Nile Hypothesis. Theoretical framework for Saharan-to-Nile migration and the 25-element testing protocol integrated into the Advanced Statistics suite.

üåã Geochemical & Tectonic Standards
    McDonough, W. F., & Sun, S. -s. (1995): Composition of the Earth. Chemical Geology. This study provides the Primitive Mantle and Chondrite normalization values used in the Spider/REE Diagrams plugin.
    Pearce, J. A., & Cann, J. R. (1973): Tectonic setting of basic volcanic rocks determined using trace element analyses. Earth and Planetary Science Letters. Provides the coordinate boundaries for the Ti-Zr-Y discrimination diagrams.
    Wood, D. A. (1980): The application of a Th-Hf-Ta diagram to problems of tectonomagmatic classification. Earth and Planetary Science Letters. Source for the Th-Hf-Ta (or substituted Nb/Zr) discrimination boundaries.
    Shervais, J. W. (1982): Ti-V plots and the petrogenesis of modern and ophiolitic lavas. Earth and Planetary Science Letters. Source for the Ti-V tectonic setting polygons.
